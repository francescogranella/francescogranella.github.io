Francesco Granella

I am a Post-Doc at RFF-CMCC European Institute on Economics and the Environment (EIEE). I received my Ph.D. in Public Policy from Bocconi University. Between September 2021 and April 2022 I was a visiting scholar at the University of California at Davis.

My work explores the economic costs of climate change and environmental stressors, in particular air pollution.

You can contact me at francesco [dot] granella [at] eiee.org
